# GenSpark Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 18, 2026

---

## Introduction

Welcome to GenSpark! We're committed to protecting your privacy while helping you learn programming. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

GenSpark is an educational mobile application designed to help students and learners master programming languages including C, C++, Java, Python, JavaScript, SQL, HTML, and CSS through interactive lessons, practice problems, and coding exercises.

By using GenSpark, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you don't agree with any part of this policy, please don't use the app.

---

## Information We Collect

When you use GenSpark, we collect the following information:

### 1. **Account Information**
- Your name
- Your email address
- Authentication method (email login or Google Sign-In)

### 2. **Learning and Progress Data**
- Lessons you start, complete, or revisit
- Time spent on each lesson
- Progress through different programming courses
- Quiz and practice problem submissions
- Your code submissions and results
- Practice problems you've attempted
- Performance scores and accuracy rates

### 3. **Certificate Data**
- Course completion records
- Certificate generation and download history
- Certificate validity information

### **What We Do NOT Collect**
We do NOT collect or store:
- Passwords (authentication is handled securely)
- Payment or credit card information
- Biometric data (fingerprints, face recognition)
- Location data
- Device identifiers or tracking cookies
- Any other sensitive personal information

---

## How We Use User Information

We use the information we collect for these purposes only:

### **For Learning & Education**
- Display your progress and completed lessons
- Personalize your learning experience
- Recommend appropriate next lessons based on your progress
- Track which topics need more practice

### **For Practice Tracking**
- Store your practice problem submissions
- Show you your attempt history
- Calculate your performance metrics
- Help you identify areas for improvement

### **For Certificate Generation**
- Verify course completion
- Generate course completion certificates with your name
- Maintain records of certificates you've earned
- Allow you to download and share your achievements

### **For Service Improvement**
- Analyze how you use GenSpark to improve the app
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Add new features based on user needs
- Monitor app performance and security

### **We Will NOT**
- Sell or share your data with third parties for marketing
- Use your learning data for purposes beyond education
- Share your code submissions with other users
- Display your personal information publicly without permission
- Use your data for commercial purposes

## Data Storage and Security

### **Where Your Data is Stored**
Your information is stored securely using:
- Cloud-based services (Supabase and similar trusted providers)
- Encrypted databases with industry-standard security protocols
- Secure servers located in protected data centers

### **How We Protect Your Data**
We take security seriously:
- All data is encrypted during transmission (SSL/TLS encryption)
- Passwords and sensitive information are never stored in plain text
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access to your data is restricted to authorized team members only
- We follow industry best practices for data protection

### **Data Retention**
- Your account data is retained while your account is active
- Progress and certificate data is retained for educational records
- If you delete your account, your data is removed within 30 days
- We retain minimal anonymous data for app improvement purposes

## Your Rights and Choices

### **Access Your Data**
You have the right to:
- Request a copy of all your personal data
- See what information we have about you
- Export your learning progress and certificates
- Contact us for any data access requests

### **Update Your Information**
You can:
- Update your name or email in account settings
- Change your learning preferences
- Modify notification settings

### **Delete Your Account**
You have the right to:
- Delete your GenSpark account at any time
- Request removal of all your personal data
- Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
- Note: You will lose access to your certificates and progress records

### **Opt-Out Options**
You can:
- Disable notifications from the app
- Opt out of non-essential emails
- Decline optional data collection features
- Delete your account without providing a reason

## Data for Minors

GenSpark is designed for educational purposes and may be used by minors:

- Parents or guardians have the same rights to access, update, and delete a minor's data
- We collect only information necessary for learning
- We do not knowingly collect unnecessary personal information from minors
- If we learn a user is under 13 and their parent hasn't consented, we will delete the account
- For users under 18, we recommend parental involvement in their learning journey

If you're a parent or guardian, you can:
- Review your child's progress anytime
- Delete your child's account
- Contact us with concerns about data collection

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does GenSpark sell my learning data?**  
A: No. We never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

**Q: Can I download my progress and certificates?**  
A: Yes. You can export your certificates and request a complete copy of your learning progress by contacting support@genspark.app.

**Q: What happens to my data if I delete my account?**  
A: All your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. Your certificates cannot be recovered after deletion.

**Q: Is my code submission safe and private?**  
A: Yes. Your code submissions are stored securely and are completely private. Other users cannot view your submissions.

**Q: Can I use GenSpark with my Google account?**  
A: Yes. Google Sign-In is optional and secure. Your Google account information is not shared with other apps or services.

**Q: How often do you update this privacy policy?**  
A: We update our Privacy Policy as needed to reflect changes in our services. We notify users of significant changes via email.
